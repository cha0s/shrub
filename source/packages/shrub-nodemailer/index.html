Sandbox#inlineCss

CREDIT: http://devintorr.es/blog/2010/05/26/turn-css-rules-into-inline-style-attributes-using-jquery/ with some improvements, of course.

  sandbox.inlineCss = (html) ->
    for selector, cssText of selectors
      for element in $(selector, $(html))
        element.style.cssText += cssText
